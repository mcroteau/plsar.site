PLSA.R is an Open Source Framework for System Development. We are going to try to simplify your life, if you are in the Java World and have experience with Servlets and JSPs then this will be a breeze in comparison and we would like to make an even bolder attempt at simplifying your life if you have developed .war applications. When I say container development I am not referring to Docker or Kubernetes but Jetty, Tomcat, Undertow or Wildfly.

We feel that writing code is the best way to get a feel for something so we are instead of boring you with verbiage, we instead are going to give you a high level overview by writing an example app.

In this application we will give the user the ability to simply add activities to a list.
